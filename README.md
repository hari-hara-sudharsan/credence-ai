## The Vision: A New Era of Financial Trust

Credence AI transforms the way financial trust is established, moving from isolated Web2 scores to programmable, verifiable, and composable Web3 intelligence. We do not just build a feature; we provide a massive, reusable infrastructure layer where anyone can build trusting financial applications without relying on opaque centralized authorities.

### Dual Architecture
The system relies on a strict separation of concerns:
- **Intelligence (Off-chain):** Python AI agents process high-fidelity financial data and risk signals.
- **Enforcement (On-chain):** Solidity smart contracts execute the final actions natively on HashKey, guided by cryptographically signed oracle attestations.

### The Full Stack
Credence AI combines a robust Indexer, a scalable backend, a real-time event pipeline, and a modular set of smart contracts to offer:
- **Credit Passport V2:** Your portable, soulbound financial identity.
- **Trust Marketplaces & Protocols:** Integrate Credence scores directly into third-party dApps.
- **P2P Institutional Lending:** Frictionless borrowing and lending backed by real data.
- **Reputation Dynamics:** Your actions immediately affect your persistent trust score.
